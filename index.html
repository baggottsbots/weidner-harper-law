About the Firm

Military values. Lowcountry roots. Since 1997.

Weidner & Harper, LLC was founded by two Marine veterans who built a law firm around service, accountability, and respect. For nearly three decades, the firm has stood with Beaufort families, service members, and local businesses with practical advice and determined representation.

Our approach reflects the values that shaped our founders: honor in every interaction, courage in the courtroom, and integrity in every result. We proudly serve a community that values tradition, family, and hard-earned trust.
